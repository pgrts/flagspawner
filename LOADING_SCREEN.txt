                      FLAGSPAWN (PD FUEL)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

              RAID THE ENEMY TAP. FUEL YOUR TEAM.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                         HOW TO PLAY
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ► Touch the ENEMY TAP in their base to grab points
  ► Bring points back to YOUR CAPTURE ZONE to score
  ► Team with the most points when time runs out WINS

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                           FUEL
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ► Capturing and returning points fills your team's FUEL
  ► Higher Fuel = BIGGER pickups at your Spawner
  ► Watch the glowing digits — they show Fuel levels

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                          WARNING
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ► Taking damage while carrying makes you DROP points
  ► Dying scatters your points for anyone to grab
  ► Don't get greedy — deliver what you have!

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                           TIPS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  ► Glowing pickups are the most valuable — prioritize them
  ► Flickering glow = pickup about to return to its team
  ► Each tap extends the round by +3 seconds
